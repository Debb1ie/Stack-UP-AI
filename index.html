About CCIS

The College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS) of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines is a premier institution dedicated to producing globally competitive IT professionals.

CCIS offers programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, and Information Systems — equipping students with technical excellence, critical thinking, and a strong foundation in computing and digital innovation.

As part of PUP's mandate to deliver quality education to deserving Filipinos, CCIS continuously bridges the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands through relevant seminars, research, and community engagement.

What You'll Gain

Seminar Objectives

This seminar equips PUP CCIS students with a practical and industry-aligned understanding of autonomous AI systems — from architecture to deployment to compliance.

01

Cloud-Native Deployment

Develop competencies in deploying AI systems using cloud-native infrastructure — containers, orchestration, and scalable architecture patterns used in production.

02

Agent Architecture

Master the building blocks of autonomous agents — memory systems, planning modules, tool-use frameworks, and action loops for real-world task completion.

03

System Evaluation

Build critical frameworks for evaluating AI systems in real-world environments — accuracy, robustness, fairness, and production-readiness benchmarks.

04

Data Privacy & Compliance

Understand Philippine data privacy laws and global AI regulations — ensuring AI deployments align with legal and ethical obligations.

05

AI Governance & Ethics

Strengthen ethical responsibility through awareness of governance gaps, bias mitigation, and responsible design principles for AI practitioners.

06

Post-Quantum Security

Gain forward-looking insights into post-quantum cryptography and the evolving security landscape for AI systems in the next decade.
